Common Questions

Does AI Insight read full-text articles?

No. AI Insight uses PubMed abstract text and metadata only. It does not access subscription-based or full-text content.

Can I edit the PubMed query directly?

Yes. Advanced Search allows manual editing of query strings and PubMed tags.

How do I suggest a new topic?

Within the app, tap the lightbulb icon in the topic selection screen to submit a suggestion. Approved updates are incorporated automatically in future app updates.

Is patient data stored?

No. CardioThoraxia does not collect or store patient information.
