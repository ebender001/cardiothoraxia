Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Overview

CardioThoraxia is designed to help clinicians perform structured PubMed literature searches. We respect your privacy and are committed to minimizing data collection.

Information We Collect

CardioThoraxia does not collect personally identifiable information.

  • No account creation is required.
  • No personal health information is collected.
  • No patient data is transmitted.

Search Data

Search queries are sent to PubMed (National Library of Medicine) to retrieve results. CardioThoraxia does not store or sell search data.

AI Insight

AI Insight is generated using PubMed abstract text and metadata only (no full-text articles). No user-identifiable information is transmitted as part of this process.

Third-Party Services

The app may rely on:

  • PubMed APIs (National Library of Medicine)
  • Secure AI processing services
  • Apple App Store services

Data Security

All communications are encrypted via HTTPS.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please visit the Support page.
